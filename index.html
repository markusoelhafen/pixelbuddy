Pixelbuddy

Hello there!
Pixelbuddy is a Figma based pixel character builder. You can build and customize your own pixelated mini-you.

Open the template in Figma

If you don't have a Figma accoount, join here for free.

Features

So many options!

Choose from a wide selection of predefined options. Shirts, pants, hair, accesories. Angry faces, sad faces, tired faces, you name it. And there is even more to come in the future!

So many colors!

Simply choose from the lots and lots of predefined colors. Shades and lightning automatically adjust. And if you want your custom color – go ahead, this is still Figma, a design tool with ALL the colors.

FAQs

In Figma, components are found in the ‘Assets’ tab in the top left corner.

If the toggle is not yet open, click on ‘Local components’ and the toggles (like clothing, head & hair) options in there. Once everything is opened you see little previews of the components. Switch between list and tile view via the switch next to the search.
